# Acme Corp — Employee Handbook (Excerpt)

**Version:** 2026.1
**Effective date:** 2026-01-01
**Owner:** People Operations (HR)
**Classification:** Internal

## 1. Working at Acme (remote-first)

Acme Corp is a **remote-first** company. Employees may work from anywhere within an approved
country where Acme has an employing entity or PEO relationship.

- **Core collaboration hours:** 10:00–14:00 in the employee's local time zone overlap with their
  team. Outside of core hours, schedules are flexible.
- **Home-office stipend:** A one-time **$1,000** setup stipend plus **$75/month** for internet
  and utilities.
- **Hub offices:** Austin, Denver, and Lisbon are available for optional in-person work.
- **Co-working:** Employees without a nearby hub may expense a co-working space up to **$300/month**.
- **In-person gatherings:** Each team meets in person at least **twice per year**; the whole
  company gathers once a year at the annual offsite.

## 2. Paid time off (PTO)

| Benefit | Amount |
| --- | --- |
| **Vacation days** | **20 days per year** (accrued monthly) |
| Additional after 4 years tenure | +5 days (**25 days/year**) |
| Company holidays | **12 days per year** (regional) |
| Sick leave | **10 days per year** |
| Parental leave | **16 weeks** paid (all new parents) |
| Bereavement | Up to 5 days |
| Volunteer time | 2 days per year |

- Vacation accrues monthly and **up to 5 unused days** may carry over into the next year.
- PTO requests are submitted in the HR system and approved by the employee's manager.

## 3. Benefits

- Medical, dental, and vision coverage (Acme pays **100%** of the employee premium, **70%** for
  dependents).
- **401(k)** with a 4% company match (US); regional pension equivalents elsewhere.
- Annual **$1,500 learning & development** budget per employee.
- Mental-health support via Modern Health.

## 4. Equipment & security

Employees receive a company laptop that must comply with the Information Security Policy
(SEC-001): full-disk encryption, MFA, and managed EDR. Report lost or stolen devices to
`security@acme.example` immediately.

## 5. Code of conduct

All employees are expected to act with integrity and treat colleagues, customers, and partners
with respect. Harassment and discrimination are not tolerated. Concerns can be raised
confidentially to People Operations or via the anonymous ethics line.

## 6. Expenses

Standard business expenses are reimbursed within 15 days of an approved report. Travel should be
booked through the company travel tool; economy fare is standard for flights under 6 hours.
