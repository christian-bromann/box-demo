# Master Services Agreement — Acme Corp & CloudVault, Inc.

**Agreement type:** Master Services Agreement (MSA) + Order Form
**Parties:** Acme Corp ("Customer") and CloudVault, Inc. ("Vendor")
**Service:** Encrypted backup and object storage for the FlowState platform
**Classification:** Confidential

## Key commercial terms

| Term | Value |
| --- | --- |
| Effective date | **2024-03-01** |
| Initial term | **36 months** (through 2027-02-28) |
| Renewal | **Auto-renews for successive 12-month terms** unless either party gives notice |
| Renewal / non-renewal notice | **90 days** before the end of the then-current term |
| **Next renewal date** | **2027-03-01** |
| Annual fee | **$240,000 / year** ($20,000 / month) |
| Payment terms | Net 30 |
| Annual price increase cap | **5%** per renewal term |

## Liability

- **Limitation of liability:** Each party's aggregate liability is capped at **the greater of
  $500,000 or the fees paid by Customer in the 12 months preceding the claim** (the
  "**liability cap**").
- **Exclusions from the cap:** The cap does **not** apply to (a) breaches of confidentiality,
  (b) data-security breaches, (c) indemnification obligations, or (d) gross negligence or willful
  misconduct. For these, liability is uncapped.
- Neither party is liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages.

## Service levels

| Metric | Commitment |
| --- | --- |
| Uptime SLA | **99.9%** monthly |
| Service credits | 10% of monthly fee per 0.1% below SLA, up to 50% |
| Support response (critical) | 1 hour, 24×7 |

## Data protection & security

- Vendor encrypts all stored data at **AES-256** and supports customer-managed keys.
- Vendor maintains **SOC 2 Type II** certification for the term of the agreement.
- Vendor acts as a **subprocessor** under Acme's DPA; sub-subprocessor changes require 30 days'
  notice.
- On termination, Vendor returns or certifies destruction of Acme data within **30 days**.

## Termination

- **For convenience:** Either party may terminate with **90 days'** written notice, effective at
  end of term (no early-termination refund for prepaid fees).
- **For cause:** Either party may terminate for material breach uncured after **30 days'** notice.
- **Effect:** Vendor provides a 60-day transition-assistance period on request.
